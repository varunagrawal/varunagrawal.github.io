I am a Padawan (a.k.a. PhD student) in Computer Science under Jedi masters Irfan Essa and James Hays in the School of Interactive Computing at Georgia Tech (Go Jackets!).

My areas of research are Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Robotics.

Before following the ways of the Force, I was a Masters student in Computer Science at Georgia Tech where I worked on multiple projects which included helping computers understand attributes of objects in images, and enabling robots to track industrial parts in real time. I also wrote some industrial robot drivers to make working with them easier.

In a previous life, I spent a stint at Microsoft India R&D Pvt. Ltd., Hyderabad, where I contributed to the Bing search engine and Enterprise Volume Licensing pipeline. I completed my undergrad (B.Tech) from NIT Surat where I was president of the ACM Chapter and Chief Editor of the college newsletter, Renesa.

In my spare time, enjoy reading, basketball, music, and hacking. Do check out my blog for my latest musings, where I try to elucidate some pretty crazy science into regular tea-time reading.
