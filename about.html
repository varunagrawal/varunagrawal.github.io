About Me

I am Varun Agrawal, a Computer Scientist, Programmer and all-round Hacker! I love to learn new things and build cool stuff using my newly acquired knowledge.

A graduate of the National Institute of Technology at Surat, I currently work at Microsoft India R&D Pvt. Ltd., Hyderabad. While in college, I organized some super sweet computing events as the President of the ACM Student Chapter (alongside my awesome team), as well as spent a year as Chief Editor of the college newsletter. I have also appeared on radio for an interview for my college's techfest in my 1st year!

My interests are in Artificial Intelligence, Operating Systems and Computer Security. These interests have lead to a Exam Rank To College ML Classifier (which is due for a patent), a Facebook Birthday-wish replying bot, a 8085 microprocessor assembler, a win in Microsoft's All India Capture The Flag 2014 event and a still-in-progress algorithm to index music based on concepts in their lyrics.

I enjoy reading, writing, basketball and music (I frequently have in-depth discussions of music with friends and have been a guitarist in various local bands), video games and anime. You can read what I am upto on my tech blog or my own musings and creativity.
