About Me


I am Varun Agrawal, a Computer Scientist, Programmer and all-round Hacker! I love to learn new things and build cool stuff using my newly acquired knowledge.

My interests are in Artificial Intelligence, Operating Systems and Computer Security. These interests have led to an Exam Rank To College ML Classifier (which is due for a patent), a Facebook Birthday-wish replying bot, a 8085 microprocessor assembler, 1st and 3rd place successively in Microsoft's All India Capture The Flag event and a due-to-be-published/patented algorithm to index music based on concepts in their lyrics.

A graduate of the National Institute of Technology at Surat, I spent a stint at Microsoft India R&D Pvt. Ltd., Hyderabad, where I contributed to the Bing search engine and Enterprise Volume Licensing pipeline. While in college, I organized some uber sweet computing events as the President of the ACM Student Chapter (alongside my awesome team), as well as spent a year as Chief Editor of the college newsletter. I have also appeared on radio for an interview for my college's techfest in my 1st year!

I enjoy reading, writing, basketball and music (I frequently have in-depth discussions of music with friends and have been a guitarist in various local bands), video games and anime. You can read what I am upto on my tech blog or my own musings and creativity.
